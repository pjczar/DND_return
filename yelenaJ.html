Yelena Jacobs of Sandpoint

A bookworm born and raised in sandpoint, her mother is the town librarian and father is an author historian. Yalena found she was magically capable and attended a wizard school in magnimar until her 18th birthday. She has since been gathering books to send to her mother library and help her fathers research.
